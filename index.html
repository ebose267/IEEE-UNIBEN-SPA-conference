About the Event

Engineering meets opportunity.
In one room.

The SPA Tech Conference 2026 is the flagship event of the IEEE UNIBEN Student Branch — a full-day technical conference designed to bridge the gap between what engineering students learn in the classroom and what the profession demands of them the day after graduation.

Sessions spanning emerging technologies, embedded systems, career development, and live networking with senior IEEE members — delivered by speakers drawn from IEEE's global network and UNIBEN's own faculty.

Every session was designed with one outcome in mind: students leaving knowing exactly what IEEE can do for their career, and motivated to make it happen.

Why Attend

Six reasons you should
be in that room.

01
Global Network Access
IEEE connects you to 400,000+ engineers worldwide. SPAx is your entry point into that community — in person, not on a screen.
02
Career Clarity
Sessions dedicated to showing you the exact path from student → graduate → professional, with specific tools and steps.
03
Meet the Speakers
Not behind glass. The networking session gives you direct access to speakers from IEEE's global network. Bring questions.
04
Technical Depth
Sessions on embedded systems and emerging technologies that extend what your 300 and 400-level courses can fit into a semester.
05
It's Free
No registration fee. No hidden cost. Show up, learn, connect, and leave with more than you came with. That's the deal.
06
Certificate of Participation
Every registered attendee receives an official IEEE UNIBEN certificate — something real for your portfolio and LinkedIn profile.
